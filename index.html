Welcome to Moran Higher secondary school.

Moran Higher Secondary School is situated at Moran town of Sivsagar district. Since 1940 this school has been contributing much to the field of education. This school is one of the biggest school in Assam with about 2000 (two thousand) students, of which more than 900 (nine hundred) students are in senior secondary section with four streams, viz. Science, Arts, Commerce and Vocational.
